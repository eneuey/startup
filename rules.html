    • Have you every wanted to enjoy a strategy game with your friends? Games like tic tac toe are too simple, and games like chess have too steep of a learning curve, but gobblet is the perfect balance of both!

    Each player gets two goblets of each size, and takes turns either putting a new goblet on the board or moving one that is already on the board. Larger goblets can cover up smaller ones, but if you move the larger goblet the smaller one will remain under it. The game ends when a player gets three goblets in a row.
